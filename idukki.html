Welcome to Idukki

Idukki, a landlocked district of Kerala with more than half of the area covered with forest. Idukki is one of the major tourist attraction of kerala for it is fully loaded with nature.

Idukki popularly known as 'The Spice Garden of Kerala'. The Asia's first and World's second great arch dam is in Idukki.

It is not only a hill station but also a place which have other attractions like wildlife sancturies, spice plantation tours, jungle walk and safaris and lots more.

Anamudi, which is the highest peak in India, south of the Himalayas, is also located in Idukki.
